# Sadbhav Hospital — Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

**Name:** Sadbhav Hospital  
**Specialty:** Pulmonology, Chest Medicine, Critical Care  
**Doctor:** Dr. Vivek Nanda  
**Location:** Jamnagar, Gujarat  

## Brand Positioning
A trustworthy, modern specialist hospital. Not a corporate chain — a dedicated expert practice with personal care at its core. The brand should feel professional, calm, and reassuring.

## Logo Usage
- Logo file: `branding/logos/` (add vector `.svg` when available)
- Current: `frontend/public/sadbhav_transparent.png`
- On white/light backgrounds: use natural colour version
- On dark/navy backgrounds: use white-inverted version (CSS: `brightness-0 invert`)
- Minimum size: 32px height
- Do not distort, recolour, or add effects

## Visual Language
- **Photography:** Real clinical settings. No stock photos of generic patients. Clean, well-lit.
- **Illustrations:** None. The brand uses photography and clean UI only.
- **Icons:** Lucide React icon set (line icons, `strokeWidth={1.8}`)
- **Spacing:** Generous white space. Never cluttered.
- **Tone:** Clinical confidence with human warmth. Clear, direct, never cold.

## Voice & Tone
- Professional but not stiff
- Compassionate, not emotional
- Informative, not overwhelming
- No excessive medical jargon in patient-facing copy
- No AI-sounding phrasing ("Dedicated to excellence in...", "State-of-the-art...")
